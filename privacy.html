Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026

Overview

After the Rainfall is a single-player picture puzzle game. This policy explains what information may be stored locally, requested through Apple system permissions, or processed by third-party advertising and security SDKs integrated in the app.

Developer Contact

Developer account and privacy contact: hengbozail@163.com

Information Stored on Your Device

The game stores progress and preferences locally using iOS user defaults. This may include:

  • Highest unlocked level.
  • Star ratings earned for completed levels.
  • Music, sound effect, and vibration settings.

This local game data is used to resume gameplay and remember settings. The game does not require an account login.

App Tracking Transparency and Advertising

The app may request permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before advertising SDK setup. If you allow tracking, the app may access the device advertising identifier for advertising-related purposes. If you deny tracking, the app continues to work and advertising setup proceeds without an IDFA.

The app includes advertising SDK integrations for Chartboost, Yandex Mobile Ads, Vungle Ads, and InMobi. These SDKs may process device, ad interaction, diagnostics, and network information according to their own privacy practices. Ad units are loaded from a dynamic configuration endpoint before supported advertising platforms are activated.

Security SDK

The app includes HSSecurity on device builds. The security SDK is initialized after the ATT decision and may use app, device, and integrity-related signals to protect the app environment and advertising flow.

Network Access

The core puzzle gameplay is designed for offline play. Network access may be used for advertising SDK initialization, ad loading, dynamic ad configuration, and related security or diagnostic operations.

Children's Privacy

The app is a casual puzzle game and does not provide account registration or direct messaging. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a third-party service used by the app, contact us so we can review the request.

Data Retention and Choices

Local progress and settings remain on the device until changed in the app, cleared by the app, or removed by uninstalling the app. You can manage tracking permission in iOS Settings at any time.

Third-Party Services

Third-party SDK providers may collect and process information under their own policies. Please review the privacy policies for Chartboost, Yandex Mobile Ads, Vungle Ads, and InMobi for more information about their data practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when app functionality, SDK integrations, or legal requirements change. The effective date above reflects the latest version of this page.
