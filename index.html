After the Rain

A calm rotating picture puzzle about restoring each scene one careful piece at a time.

Game Overview Privacy Policy

Picture Puzzle Play

Drag pieces from the tray, place them on the board, and tap to rotate. A level is complete only when every piece is in the right slot and facing the right direction.

  1. Choose a level or continue from your highest unlocked stage.
  2. Study the picture, place each tile, and rotate it into position.
  3. Finish before time runs low to earn more stars.

What Is Included

50 Levels

Progress through larger grids and stricter goals as the puzzle set grows more demanding.

Helpful Tools

Use limited previews and hints when the restored picture needs one more clue.

Local Progress

Unlocked levels, star records, music, sound, and vibration preferences are stored on your device.
